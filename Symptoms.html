What to do if you have symptoms?



  • Stay home and self-isolate if you have minor symptoms such as cough, headache, mild fever, until you recover. Call your health care provider or hotline for advice. Have someone bring you supplies. If you need to leave your houseor have someone near you, wear a medical mask to avoid infecting others.

  • If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention immediately. Call by telephone first, if you can and follow the directions your local health authority.

  • Keep up to date on the latest information from trusted sources, such as WHO on your local and national health authorities. Local and national authorities and public health units are the best place to advise on what people in your area should be doing to protect themselves.
